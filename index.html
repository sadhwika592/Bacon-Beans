ABOUT US

It's true that Bacon&Beans buys and roasts premium whole bean coffees. That's the essence of what we do, but it doesn't really cover everything. Our coffee shops have become into a landmark for coffee enthusiasts worldwide. Why do they steadfastly choose Bacon&Beans? Because they are aware that they can depend on sincere assistance, a warm setting, and consistently excellent coffee that has been carefully roasted and richly brewed. You Can't Just Expect Coffee We are fervent suppliers of coffee and all the other elements that make up a fulfilling café experience. Additionally, we provide a variety of excellent pastries, premium teas, and other mouthwatering sweets. The songs you hear in stores are also picked for their artistic merit and appeal.
People visit Bacon&Beans to work, socialise, and chat. We are a popular gathering spot for the community and a staple of daily life, and we couldn't be more delighted about it. Discover us, and you'll realise that we are much more than just the brews we produce.
